Typical attack flow (forensics view)

Reconnaissance — scanning, credential harvesting
Initial Access — phishing, vulnerable service, trojanized update
Establish Persistence — services, scheduled tasks, implants
Lateral Movement — credential reuse, remote exec
Objective — exfiltration, encryption, sabotage
This flow is what forensics aims to reconstruct from logs and artifacts.
